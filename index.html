Personal Income Tax and National Insurance Calculator

How to use

This calculator was designed with the purpose of giving the user a breif breakdown on how much they are liable to pay towards their Personal Income Tax, and National Insurance contributions.

This calculator works, by taking your hourly pay, weekly hours worked, and from there it will automatically calculate everything for you.

  • Simply enter your hourly pay, e.g £11.20 (only enter numbers and a decimal point)
  • Then below that, enter your weekly hours, e.g 35 (again, only numbers) then press submit.
  • Below in the results section you will see a total break down with an end estimate of your take home pay after deduction.

Press the submit button for results.
